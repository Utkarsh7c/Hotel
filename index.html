about us

Why are we here?
PGs & Hostels aren’t always hip. Owners and Brokers aren’t always reliable. So we’re changing the game—with spaces that inspire and delight, that fulfil needs without sacrificing style, all while delivering seamless, personalised experiences. From Virtual visits to Check in, request house-keeping and laundry, and get dinner recommendations at partner outlets with exclusive discounts—all from your phone. By eliminating inefficiencies as we scale and grow, we deliver an impeccable service and experience that’s both remarkable and accessible for all. Because everyone deserves to afford an extraordinary place to stay.

Where we’re going What began as a journey to provide a better stay has grown into a celebration of modern design and exceptional comfort with unmatched convenience. We’re working with world-class builders, architects and designers to create truly remarkable spaces—ones that transcend living experiences and enrich lives.
